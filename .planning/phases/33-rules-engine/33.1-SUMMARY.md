# Phase 33.1 — D-05 Reuse Threshold Calibration

Closed the single Phase 33 UAT gap by re-classifying D-05 from user-locked
to calibrated and running a Wave-2-style F1-max sweep against a paraphrase
fixture. Result: REUSE_SIMILARITY_THRESHOLD lowered 0.80 → 0.70.

Live UAT after fix: **17/17 PASS** (was 16/17).

## Calibration Method

**Fixture:** `modules/pathfinder/tests/fixtures/rules_reuse_calibration.json`
8 anchors × (3 paraphrases + 2 same-topic-different-question candidates)
= **40 labeled pairs** covering flanking, off-guard, demoralize, spellcasting,
healing, falling, dying, critical hits.

**Script:** `modules/pathfinder/scripts/calibrate_reuse_threshold.py`
Mirrors `calibrate_retrieval_threshold.py` but operates on (anchor, candidate)
pairs. Embeds 48 unique queries via LM Studio, computes cosine for all 40 pairs,
sweeps 13 candidate thresholds (0.50..0.85), picks F1-max with **precision tie-break**
(false-reuse serves stale-answer-to-different-question — worse than missed-reuse
where the LLM just composes fresh).

**Embedding model:** `text-embedding-nomic-embed-text-v1.5` (768-dim, via LM Studio)

## Separation Diagnostic

| Class | n | min | mean | max |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Paraphrase (should reuse) | 24 | 0.6619 | **0.7868** | 0.8925 |
| Different (should NOT reuse) | 16 | 0.5185 | **0.7185** | 0.8122 |

**Overlap band: [0.6619, 0.8122]** — both classes coexist in this range.

The mean gap is 0.07 — the embedding model does not separate paraphrases from
same-topic-different-question pairs cleanly. No threshold gives both high
precision and high recall on this model. Future model-swap data point.

## Future Work — When To Reconsider

The 0.07 mean separation between paraphrases and same-topic-different-question
pairs is the empirical floor of `text-embedding-nomic-embed-text-v1.5` for this
domain. Signals that would re-trigger this calibration:

1. **Real DM usage shows high false-reuse rate** — if users complain "the bot keeps
   serving me an old answer that doesn't quite match my question," the precision
   floor (0.72 at chosen threshold) is the bottleneck. Swap embedding model.

2. **Real DM usage shows high missed-reuse rate** — if users complain "I asked the
   same question two different ways and the bot composed fresh both times," the
   threshold may be too high. But at 0.70 with recall=0.958, this is unlikely.

3. **Embedding model swap** — any change to `settings.rules_embedding_model` (e.g.
   to `bge-large-en-v1.5`) MUST trigger recalibration. The fixture and script are
   reusable; just rerun `calibrate_reuse_threshold.py`.

4. **Corpus scope expansion** — if D-15 scope expands beyond Player Core (e.g. to
   Player Core 2, Monster Core), the topic distribution shifts and the fixture
   may need new anchors covering the new scope.

The calibration script is committed and rerunnable — the discipline is preserved.
